Since you’re here,
you care. You care about the health of future generations.
Did you know you can contribute directly with very little effort?

Here’s how

There are diseases that we know about, and that affect millions of people around the world today,but that we have no cure for. The science community is working very hard to find cures and one of the most promising approaches is based on genetic research.

For this to work, science needs a tiny bit of genetic information from a lot of people. This is where you can help by giving a tiny bit of your genetic information.

We promise

To store it safely and securely

To give you complete control over
who has access to it

To give you complete control over
what is done with it
